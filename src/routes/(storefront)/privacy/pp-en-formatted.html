Privacy Policy

JWP Consulting GK (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") is intent on protecting the privacy of any user of the Company's website(s) and the Company’s service(s) (hereinafter collectively referred to as “Website, Etc.”) (such a user is hereinafter referred to as “User”) with respect to handling the Personal Information, etc. in the Website, Etc. The Company would like the User to read this privacy policy (hereinafter referred to as "Privacy Policy") carefully and thoroughly before using the Website, Etc.

Article 1 (Definitions)

The terms used in the Privacy Policy are defined as follows:

  1. "Personal Information" means any personal information under the Personal Information Protection Law, i.e., any information about a living individual that includes a name, date of birth, address, telephone number, contact information, or any other description that identifies a specific individual or includes a personal identification code.

  2. "Personal Information Database, Etc." means a personal information database, etc. under the Personal Information Protection Law, i.e., a collection of information containing Personal Information that is systematically organized so that specific Personal Information can be retrieved by using a computer, and any other one specified by a Cabinet Order.

  3. "Personal Data" means any personal data under the Personal Information Protection Law, i.e., any Personal Information that constitutes Personal Information Databases, Etc.

Article 2 (Scope of the Privacy Policy)

  1. The Privacy Policy shall be applied between the Company and the User with respect to the handling of Personal Information, etc. in the Website, Etc.

  2. In case the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and its related national laws are applicable, the Company’s GDPR Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "GDPR Privacy Policy") shall apply in addition to the Privacy Policy.

  3. In case of any discrepancy between the Privacy Policy and the GDPR Privacy Policy, the GDPR Privacy Policy shall prevail.

Article 3 (Method of Collecting Personal Information)

The Company may ask the User to provide any Personal Information or collect any Personal Information when the User uses the Website, Etc. In addition, the Company may obtain any Personal Information from any third parties with whom the Company collaborates.

Article 4 (Purpose of Using Personal Information)

The purposes for which the Company uses any Personal Information are as follows:

  1. To provide, maintain, protect, and improve the Website, Etc. such as by accepting registrations, verifying the identity of any User, calculating usage fees, etc.;

  2. To notify the public of any information regarding the contents of the Website, Etc. any updates thereof, any events and campaigns thereof, and any changes, etc. to any terms and conditions thereon;

  3. To respond to any notice or inquiry regarding the Website, Etc.;

  4. To respond to any violation of the Company's terms and conditions or other rules and regulations concerning the Website, Etc.;

  5. To compile any statistical data on the use of the Website, Etc.;

  6. To plan, draft or implement any future activities related to the Website, Etc.;

  7. To authenticate the identity of any User when or after such a User logs in in using the Website, Etc.;

  8. To analyze any registration information and usage conditions in order to make the contents and advertisements provided by any third parties that the User sees when using the Website, Etc., be more convenient for the User;

  9. To understand the delivery status of advertisements, to measure the effectiveness of advertisements, and to display behaviorally targeted advertisements; and,

  10. To distribute or display advertisements of the Company or any third party.

Article 6 (Provision to Third Party)

  1. The Company will not provide any Personal Information to any third party without the prior consent of the User, except in the following cases:

    1. In case of being required by laws and regulations;

    2. In case it is necessary for the protection of life, body, or property of an individual and it is difficult to obtain the User’s consent;

    3. In case it is especially necessary to improve public health or to promote the sound growth of children and it is difficult to obtain the User's consent; or,

    4. In case it is necessary to cooperate with a national agency, a local government, or an individual or entity entrusted by either a national agency or local government to execute affairs prescribed by the laws and regulations, and obtaining the User's consent is likely to impede the execution of such affairs.

  2. Notwithstanding the preceding paragraph of Article 6.1, a person to whom any Personal Information is provided in the following cases shall not be considered a third party:

    1. In case the Company outsources all or part of the handling of the Personal Information within the scope necessary to achieve the purpose of use;

    2. In case the Personal Information is provided as a result of the succession of business due to merger or other reasons; or,

    3. In cases the Personal Information is used jointly with a specific person or entity, and the Company notifies or make easily available to the User such joint use, the items of the Personal Information to be jointly used, the scope of the joint users, the purpose of use by such joint users, the name and address of the person responsible for managing the relevant Personal Information, and in the case of a corporation, the name of its representative in addition to the above information.

The Company may entrust the handling of any entrusted Personal Information to any third party to the extent necessary for the provision of the Website, Etc. and for the achievement of safe and sound use by the User. These third parties will be selected after confirming that they have adequate security standards for the handling of the Personal Information, and will be supervised as necessary and appropriate through contracts and other means.

Article 9 (Security Control Measures)

The Company implements the following security control measures to ensure the proper handling of the Personal Data:

  1. to establish a basic policy regarding compliance with relevant laws, regulations, guidelines, etc., and a contact point for handling questions and complaints;

  2. to establish basic handling methods when acquiring, using, and storing the Personal Data, etc.;

  3. to establish a person responsible for the handling of the Personal Data and a reporting and communication system for the person responsible;

  4. to provide any confidentiality of the Personal Data in internal rules, etc.;

  5. to implement measures to ensure that the Personal Data cannot be easily accessed by anyone other than employees who are authorized to handle the Personal Data and the individual concerned; and,

  6. to implement mechanisms to protect equipment that handles the Personal Data from unauthorized external access or unauthorized software.
